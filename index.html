Temporal Features and machine Learning Approaches to Study Brain Activity with EEG and ECG

Electroencephalogram (EEG) and Electrocardiogram (ECG) are electrical signals that reflect activities of the brain and cardiac, respectively, based on which some neurological disorders and mental status are determined. In this paper, a novel set of temporal features like energy, Shannon energy, entropy, and temporal energy, all together along with machine learning-based classifiers to identify the relaxing state of humans and while performing mental tasks like arithmetic operations using these signals are proposed.
